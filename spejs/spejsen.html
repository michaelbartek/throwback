A production media team that brings a new visual to your property, your space!

The name Spejs comes from a phonetic word, which is the Slovak version of the English "space". This term evokes space, creativity and unlimited possibilities for innovation. We decided to use this idea and fully dedicate ourselves to shooting spaces. We are united by a passion for producing aesthetic photographs and technical videos. Our main focus is professional filming and photography of real estate. We want to give potential buyers and those interested in renting a property the right impression at first glance. Our work is about creating high-quality visual content that shows the property in its best light.
